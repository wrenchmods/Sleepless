    Welcome to my project

    Sleepless is a Horror-Adventure-Action game made by kaitek666, based on Source Engine. The game is still in development. Young, 32 years old man, Bill Brown's got very normal life with his lovely wife Alice. Both are in a good marriage, but in the last weeks, they haven't been enjoying their relationship. When Bill comes bit late home after work, he just goes to bed. But, during night, something strange happens in their house. Something, which changes Bill's life forever and makes him fight to survive.

    We can't tell you nothing more about our game, but keep checking this page to find out more about it. Hope you like it as well as I do while developing it. Have fun while shitting to your pants.
